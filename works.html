Reservoir Watch

Prototype developed by team AQRITY to predict near real-time water availability in reservoirs. Award winner in the Wave2Web Hack Global Hackathon organized by World Resources Institute (WRI), Microsoft and BlackRock.

002/005

Weather Satellite Data Reciever

Recieving low resolution images from the weather satellites such as NOAA 18, 19, 15 and Meteor M N2-3. Using a RTL-SDR dongle with a Raspberry Pi to record the VHF satellite transmissions and then decoding them to get near-real time weather and land observations.

003/005

Meteo-ADSB

App to analyze the vertical atmospheric structure by calculating temperature, altitutde and windspeed from real time aircraft data and plotting them. The ADS-B (aircraft data) decoder is live on Flightradar24.com, Flightaware.com and Opensky.org
