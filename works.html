💧 HydroSwift

A lightweight Python tool for downloading and plotting hydrology time series data from India-WRIS and CWC Flood Forecasting System. Supports discharge, water level, rainfall, temperature, humidity, solar radiation, sediment, groundwater, and atmospheric pressure datasets.

🛰️ CarbSat

A modern, minimalistic web application for real-time satellite tracking with interactive orbit visualization and pass prediction capabilities. Track hundreds of satellites on an interactive world map with real-time position updates, orbit paths, and upcoming pass predictions.

Reservoir Watch

Prototype developed by team AQRITY to predict near real-time water availability in reservoirs. Award winner in the Wave2Web Hack Global Hackathon organized by World Resources Institute (WRI), Microsoft and BlackRock.

004/007

Weather Satellite Data Receiver

Receiving low resolution images from the weather satellites such as NOAA 18, 19, 15 and Meteor M N2-3. Using a RTL-SDR dongle with a Raspberry Pi to record the VHF satellite transmissions and then decoding them to get near-real time weather and land observations.

005/007

Meteo-ADSB

App to analyze the vertical atmospheric structure by calculating temperature, altitude and windspeed from real time aircraft data and plotting them. The ADS-B decoder is live on Flightradar24.com, Flightaware.com and Opensky.org
